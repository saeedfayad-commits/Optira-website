Accountant

Help individuals and businesses manage money, minimise tax, and make smart financial decisions.

$60k–$130k
Salary (AUD)
3 yrs
Study time
Medium
AI impact
80/100
Job stability

A day in the life

  • Prepare financial statements, tax returns and budgets
  • Advise clients or management on financial decisions
  • Audit records for accuracy and compliance
  • Use accounting software to track and forecast cash flow

How to get there (NSW pathway)

Step 1 — HSC
Mathematics (Standard or Advanced), Economics or Business Studies (recommended, not required)
Step 2 — Study
Bachelor of Commerce or Bachelor of Business (Accounting major) — 3 years
Step 3 — Get qualified
Complete CA (Chartered Accountant) or CPA Australia professional program after your degree to become fully qualified

Skills that matter most

Numeracy Attention to detail Analytical thinking
